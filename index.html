CALAMITY CHASM

Calamity Chasm is a third-person casual shooter where you play as a demon named Kram, competing in a sporting coliseum surrounded by fiery pits that lead into the deeper parts of Hell. Your objective is to banish souls by shooting fireballs that deal knockback, causing them to fall into the pit. The level is filled with various traps, such as trapdoors, punching fists, and exploding barrels, which can either hinder you or be used strategically. Your goal is to survive, banish as many souls as possible, and become the best demon in Hell.

GAMEJAMS

RUN BUBBLES RUN

RUN BUBBLES RUN

Run Bubbles Run is a 3rd person, 2-player local PvP game where players control uniquely identifiable bubbles, distinguished by their underwear. The goal is to race along a beach-themed obstacle course while avoiding hazards like seashells, starfish, and swinging shovels hidden inside sandcastles. These obstacles can pop your bubble, setting you back on the track. Skillfully jump over or navigate around them to stay ahead. The first bubble to cross the finish line wins the race!

Check the game out on itch.io

COSMIC CRUISER REPAIR

COSMIC CRUISER REPAIR

Cosmic Cruiser Repair is a 2D isometric casual, simulation-puzzle game where you play as a space mechanic, repairing a range of quirky vehicles. Use the correct tool and complete a single challenging mini-game to get each vehicle back in working order. Your customers are a lively group of robots, each with a story to share as they wait for their repairs. Race against the clock to fix as many space cruisers as possible and earn big profits before time runs out!

Check the game out on itch.io
